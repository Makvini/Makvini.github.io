FAQ

Frequently

Asked

Questions

What are unlimited requests, tasks, and revisions?

This means subscribers can make as many design and development requests, assign tasks, and request revisions as needed within a billing period. This offers flexibility for clients to iterate and refine projects without additional costs for each interaction, ensuring the final deliverables meet their evolving needs.

How can I talk with my designer and developer?

Communication with the designer and developer occurs through Slack Connect. You can also add your team to the channel.

How many designs will I receive monthly?

The quantity is contingent on the complexity and the number of iterations required for each task. While each task varies, we assure you of receiving daily design updates.
